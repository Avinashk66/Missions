Traditional Filter Kaapi Recipe

  1. Heat water to 70–80°C (not boiling)
  2. Add 3 tablespoons of freshly ground coffee to the filter
  3. Slowly pour hot water in circular motions
  4. Let it brew for 5–7 minutes
  5. Press the filter rod gently to extract all liquid
  6. Add ¼ glass of hot milk to the tumbler
  7. Pour coffee from height to create froth
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy the aroma

Coffee Terminology

Filter Kaapi
Traditional South Indian coffee brewed using a metal filter.
Chikmagalur
Premium Arabica coffee from Chikmagalur district, Karnataka.
Cream
Velvety froth created by pouring coffee from height.
Decoction
Concentrated brewed coffee used as the base.
Robusta
Strong coffee variety with higher caffeine content.
